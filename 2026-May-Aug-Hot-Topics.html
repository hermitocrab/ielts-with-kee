IELTS Speaking Part 2 - Hot Topics (May-Aug 2026)

People (人物)

  • 极高想从事医药行业的人 (A person who wants to work in the medical industry)
  • 高喜欢种植物的人 (A person who likes to grow plants)

Objects (物品)

  • 高喜欢的传统物品 (A traditional object you like)
  • 高电子产品出问题 (A time your electronic device had a problem)

Events (事件)

  • 极高一个早起的经历 (An experience of getting up early)
  • 极高现场观看体育比赛 (A time you watched a live sports event)
  • 极高重要的决定 (An important decision you made)
  • 高改变决定的时刻 (A time you changed your decision)

Places (地点)

  • 极高喜欢或者不喜欢的高楼 (A tall building you like or dislike)
  • 高无聊的地方 (A boring place you have been to)
  • 高一个你去过喜欢上的城市 (A city you have visited and liked)

Abstract (抽象)

  • 极高一个有趣的生物知识 (An interesting piece of biological knowledge)
  • 极高想颁布的新法律 (A new law you would like to introduce)
  • 极高特别节日的食物 (Food you eat on a special festival)
